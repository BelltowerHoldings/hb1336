How this works

The model compares two worlds over a fixed horizon H = 10 years. Under do nothing, charity-funded deposits are one-way grants — the deposit refunds to the tenant at move-out and never returns to the fund. Under HB 1336, charity-funded deposits (and RCDs, where used) refund to the third-party payor under RSA 540-A:7, I(b) and are redeployed to the next household.

Variables — Indices & horizon

H
Modeling horizon, in years (fixed at 10 in this calculator)
t
Year index, t = 1, 2, …, H
i
Credit-tier index, i = 1, …, 5 (350–449, 450–499, 500–549, 550–649, 650–749)

Variables — Inputs

N
Annual charitable inflow (gross donations per year)
o
Overhead rate (share of N consumed by fundraising & admin)
D
Average security deposit (≤ 1 month rent under RSA 540-A:6)
m
RCD size as a multiple of D (capped at 1.0 under HB 1336)
b
Share of assisted households also needing an RCD on top of the deposit
L
Average tenancy before refund, in years
f
Recycle factor: 1 if overhead is not re-charged on recycled capital; (1 − o) otherwise
pi
Population share in credit tier i
ei
Eviction rate in credit tier i (TransUnion ResidentScore)
LGE
Loss-given-eviction (share of held capital consumed when an eviction is filed and reduced to judgment)
B
Base non-recovery (lawful deductions absent eviction — wear, cleaning, damages)

Variables — Derived quantities

N′
Net new capital per year  =  N × (1 − o)
ē
Population-weighted eviction rate  =  Σi pi × ei
r
Effective recovery rate (see formula below)
K
Capital deployed per assisted household under HB 1336  =  D × (1 + b × m)
K0
Capital deployed per assisted household under do nothing  =  D
C(t)
Capital deployed in year t under HB 1336 (see recurrence below)
C0(t)
Capital deployed in year t under do nothing  =  N′ (no recycling)
HH(t)
Households served in year t under HB 1336  =  C(t) / K
HH0
Households served per year under do nothing  =  N′ / K0  (constant across years)

Recovery rate

r  =  max( 0, 1 − ē × LGE − B )     where   ē  =  Σi pi × ei

If the recovery method is set to "Manual override," r is taken directly from that input instead.

Annual capital evolution

HB 1336:    C(t)  =  N′  +  f × r × C(t − L)     for t > L;    C(t) = N′  otherwise
Do nothing:    C0(t)  =  N′     for every t   (no recycling — recovery is 0% by definition)

Households served

HB 1336:    HH(t)  =  C(t) / K      cumulative:    Σt=1..H HH(t)
Do nothing:    HH0  =  N′ / K0    (constant across years)      cumulative:    H × HH0

Reach multiplier

Reach multiplier  =  [ Σt=1..H HH(t) ]  ÷  [ H × HH0 ]

Source data: eviction rates by credit tier are from the TransUnion ResidentScore analysis cited in the HB 1336 comprehensive policy analysis. Caveat: illustrative underwriting model, not a guarantee of outcomes. Real-world recovery and tenancy vary by program design and population.
